I'm a Senior Product Designer focused on solving complex business problems through product strategy, workflow design, and scalable user experiences. I've worked on enterprise platforms where users manage large datasets, sophisticated workflows, and critical business decisions — going beyond screens to understand systems, user behaviour, and business goals. Today I explore how AI can be embedded into workflows to automate repetitive tasks, improve decision-making, and create measurable value.

UX Thinking

Understanding User Behaviors

Conducting user interviews, observing workflows, and analyzing feedback to uncover needs, motivations, and pain points.

Defining User Problems

Synthesizing research into clear problem statements that align user challenges with business objectives.

Designing End-to-End Experiences

Mapping user journeys and designing seamless experiences across multiple touchpoints to support task completion.

Simplifying Complex Workflows

Breaking down complicated processes into intuitive steps, reducing cognitive load and improving usability.

Validating Solutions Through Research

Testing concepts and prototypes with users to gather insights and refine designs before implementation.

Driving User-Centered Decisions

Combining user insights, data, and stakeholder input to ensure product decisions are grounded in real user needs.

PM Thinking

Identifying Market Opportunities

Analyzing customer needs, industry trends, and business challenges to uncover opportunities for product innovation.

Defining Product Strategy

Connecting user problems and business goals to establish a clear direction for product development.

Balancing User, Business & Technology

Evaluating solutions through the lens of user value, business impact, and technical feasibility simultaneously.

Prioritizing High-Impact Solutions

Assessing opportunities based on effort, value, and strategic importance to focus on the most impactful initiatives.

Measuring Product Success

Delivering Business Outcomes

AI Thinking

Identifying AI Opportunities

Analyzing workflows to pinpoint where AI can automate repetition, surface timely insights, or reduce cognitive load.

Designing Human-AI Collaboration

Creating interaction patterns that pair AI capability with human judgment, keeping users informed and in control.

Mapping Agentic Workflows

Defining multi-step AI orchestration — what agents handle, when they hand off, and how humans monitor or intervene.

Specifying AI System Behaviour

Translating user needs into AI behaviour specs — what to surface, when to act, and how to handle uncertainty.

Evaluating Trust & Explainability

Designing AI outputs users can understand and verify, with appropriate confidence signals and transparency.

Balancing Automation & Human Control

Determining where AI acts autonomously versus where human oversight is essential, based on risk and context.
